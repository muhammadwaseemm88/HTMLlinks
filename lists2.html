Examples

  • Gilgit
  • Chair

Kinds of Nouns

There are many kinds of Noun. Some of them are as follow:

  1. Common nouns
  2. proper nouns
  3. Abstract nouns
  4. Collective nouns
  5. Countable nouns
  6. Uncountable nouns
